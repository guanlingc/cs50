Chocolate chip dookies with chewy oats

Ingredients

  • 300g (3 cups) old-fashioned rolled oats
  • 210g (1.5 cups) unbleached organic all-purpose flour
  • 40g (5 tablespoons) brewer's yeast
  • 18g (3 tablespoons) ground flaxseed
  • 0.5 teaspoon baking powder
  • 0.5 teaspoon baking soda
  • 0.5 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 0.25 teaspoon salt
  • 250g (2 sticks) of butter:
    • 12 tablespoons organic unsalted butter
    • 4 tablespoons unrefined organic virgin coconut oil (or butter)
    • Note: 8 tablespoons is approximately 110g, 1 stick is approximately 115g
  • 270g (1.5 cups) organic cane sugar (1 cup of sugar is just right for 1 batch, can use half brown and half white)
  • 1 large egg + 1 large egg yolk
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 240g (1.5 cups) dark chocolate chips/chunks

Optional:

  • Unsweetened flaked coconut
  • Chopped almonds
  • 1 to 2 tablespoons of almond butter

Instructions

Making the dough:

  1. Beat butter until creamy.
  2. Add sugar and combine thoroughly.
  3. Add egg.
  4. Add vanilla essence.
  5. Slowly add all dry ingredients until just combined.
  6. Stir in chocolate chips.

**Storage:** The dough can be kept refrigerator up to 48 hours for flavours to develop

**Baking:** Preheat oven to 180 degrees Celsius. Bake for 10 to 14 minutes.
